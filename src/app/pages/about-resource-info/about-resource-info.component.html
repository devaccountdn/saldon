New Home Advantages


One of the first decisions home buyers are faced with is whether to purchase a new or used home. Although there are many benefits in purchasing a used home, purchasing a new Gold Leaf home can also provide many advantages and benefits to home buyers. These benefits include:


  1. All aspects of the home are brand new. This includes the furnace, water heater, air exchanger, roof … etc. This is a great selling feature for new homes as potential home buyers can rest easy knowing that there is no chance of unexpected renovation costs after moving in, and additional financial stress!
  2. Along with the brand new features, a purchase of a new Gold Leaf home comes with the assurance that your investment will last for a very long time! All of our homes come with a lifetime warranty on the foundation, as well as a 7 year Tarion home warranty protecting home buyers against any potential defects in their new home.
  3. A new Gold Leaf home also gives home buyers the opportunity to leave their personal stamp on the place they will call home. You have complete control over your paint colours, flooring, cabinets, counter tops as well as any other features in your new home. We will work with you to create your perfect dream home!
  4. Purchasing a new Gold Leaf home also gives home buyers the assurance that they are investing in a safe and efficient future for their families. Over the years, building science continually improves as do the requirements for the Ontario Building Code. When you purchase a new Gold Leaf home, you can be assured that your home will meet all updated requirements giving you the benefit of more efficient heating, ventilation and power consumption – ultimately giving you the advantage of lower utility bills and saving you money over the years!

Home Care Guide


At Gold Leaf Developments, we won’t leave you in the dark on how to maintain your new home!


Upon closing, we will provide you with a detailed home care guide with information about all aspects of your new home, maintenance procedures as well as appropriate warranty information.


You will also receive the owner’s manual and operation booklet for any appliance we may have installed: possibly including furnace, water heater, air exchanger, dishwasher, microwave, etc.

Warranty Information


  1. The purchase of a new Gold Leaf home comes with the additional benefit of a lifetime warranty on the foundation, as well as a 7 year Tarion new home warranty against defects.
  2. At the time of sale, we will provide you with a Tarion new home warranty manual as well as the additional warranty we provide above Tarion. We will go through these manuals in detail with you explaining the contents and how to go about using the manual if need be.
  3. Before your closing date, the most senior company employee (usually the owner) will walk through your new house with you and do a detailed per-delivery inspection of your home. This will give us another opportunity to discuss your warranty and address any items before you move in to your home.
  4. 30 days after your closing date, we will send a customer service representative to your home to address any issues or defects that may have presented themselves in your first month of occupancy. This will also give you an opportunity to discuss any warranty questions you may have, or to provide us with feedback on your experience with us.
  5. Finally, 1 year after your closing date we will once again send our customer service representative to your home to address any issues or defects that may have presented themselves over the past year. You can rest assure that we will do our best to provide you with a quality home and address any issues you may have! You deserve the best!
